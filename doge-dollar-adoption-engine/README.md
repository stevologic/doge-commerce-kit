# Doge Dollar Adoption Engine

This is a static adoption and promotion command center for the only legal path that can support a higher DOGE price: real usage, merchant acceptance, transparent proof, and repeatable community execution.

The May 2026 baseline used in the tool is intentionally conservative:

- DOGE reference price: $0.1063
- Circulating supply reference: 169.74B DOGE
- Implied $1 network value: about $169.74B before future issuance
- Gap from baseline to $1: about 9.4x

Primary sources used for the baseline and adoption rails:

- CoinMarketCap DOGE page: https://coinmarketcap.com/currencies/dogecoin/
- Dogecoin Foundation: https://dogecoin.org/
- Dogebox: https://dogebox.dogecoin.org/
- BitPay supported invoice currencies: https://support.bitpay.com/hc/en-us/articles/203411543-What-cryptocurrencies-can-I-use-to-pay-a-BitPay-Invoice
- BitPay settlement options: https://support.bitpay.com/hc/en-us/articles/201890513-What-are-my-options-for-settlement

## How to use it

Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step is required.

Use the tool every week to:

1. Pick a merchant segment with a realistic Dogecoin buyer overlap.
2. Generate a lawful outreach message focused on new sales and lower-friction checkout.
3. Track contacted, onboarding, live, and proof-posted merchants.
4. Estimate the DOGE transaction demand produced by live merchant activity.
5. Publish proof posts that show DOGE being used for normal commerce without price promises.

## Strategy

The engine intentionally avoids manipulation, coordinated pumping, misleading price targets, or investment advice. It pushes a six-month campaign around measurable adoption:

- Merchant acceptance through existing processors such as BitPay or self-hosted Dogebox paths.
- Public proof of purchases and merchant case studies.
- Local and online merchant clusters where Dogecoin buyers already have cultural affinity.
- Weekly accountability metrics rather than vague promotion.

The result cannot guarantee a market price. It is designed to create the kind of real utility and demand that has the strongest legal and strategic relationship to long-term DOGE value.
