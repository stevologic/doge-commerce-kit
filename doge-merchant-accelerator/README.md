# DOGE Merchant Accelerator

This is a self-contained adoption and promotion kit for getting small merchants to accept Dogecoin legally, without price promises, market manipulation, paid shilling, or deceptive urgency.

The central bet is simple: DOGE needs visible places to spend it, repeated low-friction transactions, and merchant stories that make adoption feel practical instead of speculative. This kit creates a campaign page, a merchant ROI calculator, compliant outreach copy, launch checklists, and an embeddable "Pay with Dogecoin" badge.

## Files

- `index.html` - static campaign and merchant onboarding page.
- `styles.css` - responsive interface styling.
- `app.js` - calculator, checklist state, copy helpers, and badge generator.
- `merchant-outreach.md` - compliant outreach sequences.
- `campaign-plan.md` - six-month adoption push plan.
- `merchant-scorecard.csv` - template for ranking likely merchant wins.
- `launch-report-template.md` - structure for turning launches into credible proof.

## How to Use

Open `index.html` in a browser. No build step is required.

Use the page to:

1. Show merchants a plain-English DOGE acceptance pitch.
2. Estimate transaction volume and campaign lift without promising price impact.
3. Generate a DOGE payment badge for a merchant site.
4. Track readiness for a launch sprint.
5. Copy compliant outreach language.
6. Score merchant prospects and publish merchant-approved adoption recaps.

## Compliance Boundaries

This kit is for adoption and promotion only.

Do:

- Promote real merchant acceptance and real customer utility.
- Disclose that DOGE is volatile.
- Avoid predictions, guarantees, or coordinated trading instructions.
- Use merchant-controlled payment processors, wallets, accounting, and tax workflows.
- Measure adoption through merchant count, transaction count, and repeat use.

Do not:

- Promise DOGE will reach any price.
- Ask people to buy, hold, pump, or coordinate market activity.
- Misrepresent partnerships, merchant results, or user numbers.
- Use fake testimonials, bots, wash transactions, or undisclosed paid promotion.

## Six-Month Adoption Thesis

Reaching `$1` would require broad market demand well beyond this kit alone. The legally constructive path is to increase DOGE utility enough that media, creators, merchants, and holders have a repeatable reason to use DOGE rather than merely discuss it.

The highest-leverage sequence is:

1. Recruit merchants with audiences that already understand internet-native culture.
2. Give them an easy DOGE offer, such as a small discount, limited merch drop, or DOGE-only perk.
3. Convert every launch into public proof: merchant page, transaction count range, customer story, and setup guide.
4. Repeat weekly in clusters so adoption appears normal and practical.
5. Package results into press-friendly case studies without making investment claims.
