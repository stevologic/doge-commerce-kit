## Public Launch Guardrails

Approved phrases:

- "DOGE is now spendable here."
- "This is a real checkout option for customers who already want to use DOGE."
- "Crypto prices are volatile; this is not investment advice."
- "The campaign measures merchant adoption, not market price."

Avoid:

- "This will send DOGE to `$1`."
- "Buy before the launch."
- "Hold until merchants announce."
- "Guaranteed upside."
- "Coordinated push."
