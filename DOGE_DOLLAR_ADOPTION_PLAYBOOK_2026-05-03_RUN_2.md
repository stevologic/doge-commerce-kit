# DOGE Dollar Adoption Playbook

Run date: 2026-05-03

Objective: increase real Dogecoin utility through legal adoption and promotion only. This does not make investment claims, coordinate trading, imply guaranteed price movement, or ask anyone to buy DOGE as an investment.

## Current Read

- DOGE recently traded near $0.106 with roughly $18B market cap in CoinMarketCap results checked during this run.
- A $1 target would require a roughly 9x move from that reference price and a much larger market cap, so a credible legal strategy must change the usage narrative, not just the attention cycle.
- The fastest adoption path is merchant checkout. Dogecoin already has payment rails that can be promoted without inventing a new chain or custody product.

## Best Legal Wedge

Make DOGE spendable at many visible merchants, then ask the community to generate real purchase proof.

The core loop:

1. Recruit merchants whose buyers already overlap with DOGE culture.
2. Help them enable DOGE checkout using existing rails.
3. Publish verified checkout URLs.
4. Ask DOGE holders to spend small amounts on real goods and services.
5. Share opt-in receipts and merchant case studies.
6. Repeat by vertical until DOGE is seen as a practical payment option again.

## Target Verticals

- Creator merchandise
- Gaming communities and servers
- Sports fan shops
- Collectibles
- Food trucks and local food brands
- Independent ecommerce stores
- Digital services with international buyers

## Rail Positioning

- BitPay: hosted checkout path where DOGE invoice payments and settlement are supported. Useful for merchants that want compliance, settlement, and low engineering burden.
- NOWPayments: DOGE payment gateway with invoices, plugins, API, and donation tools. Useful for smaller businesses that want non-custodial or plugin-led checkout.
- GigaWallet: Dogecoin-native backend for businesses that can self-host and want direct DOGE transaction infrastructure.
- Coinbase Business: verify current asset support before positioning. Coinbase Commerce is transitioning into Coinbase Business and the public messaging appears stablecoin-first, so do not pitch it as a DOGE-first path without current confirmation.

## Six-Month Sprint

Month 1: public proof

- Build a list of 500 target merchants.
- Recruit 25 volunteer captains by vertical.
- Ask each captain to contact 20 merchants per week.
- Convert at least 8 percent of contacts.
- Publish live DOGE checkout links and opt-in launch receipts.

Months 2-3: repeatable vertical packets

- Convert the best-performing verticals into short case studies.
- Create reusable onboarding pages for creator merch, gaming, local food, and collectibles.
- Keep all public proof tied to real checkout pages and real purchases.

Months 4-6: scale distribution

- Expand to 25,000 merchant prospects.
- Recruit regional captains and partner communities.
- Publish weekly adoption scoreboards with merchant count, active checkout links, and opt-in order proof.
- Push integration partners to feature DOGE setup guides.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise $1, returns, or price movement.
- Do not coordinate buys, timed purchasing, wash volume, or exchange activity.
- Do not fabricate merchant adoption.
- Do not publish customer receipts without opt-in consent.
- Do not imply Dogecoin Foundation, BitPay, NOWPayments, Coinbase, or any merchant endorsement unless formally confirmed.
