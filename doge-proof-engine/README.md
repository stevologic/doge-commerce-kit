# DOGE Proof Engine

This is a legal adoption and promotion tool for DOGE Spend Week. It helps a campaign operator turn real Dogecoin purchases into usable evidence: proof intake, fraud-risk screening, merchant scorecards, compliant social copy, and weekly adoption reports.

The goal is not to promise or engineer a price outcome. The goal is to increase legitimate DOGE utility by making every real checkout event easier to verify, summarize, and reuse as merchant recruitment proof.

## Why this matters

DOGE near ten cents needs a major shift in visible demand to approach one dollar. The most defensible path is real commerce:

- more merchants accepting DOGE,
- more buyers spending DOGE,
- more repeat DOGE purchases,
- more public proof that merchants can reference,
- more payment processors and wallets amplifying the activity because the data is real.

This engine is designed for that loop.

## Files

- `index.html` - self-contained browser app for proof intake, screening, copy generation, and scorecards.
- `proof-schema.json` - machine-readable schema for purchase proof records.
- `proof-ledger-template.csv` - weekly operating ledger template.
- `operator-playbook.md` - execution workflow and publishing rules.
- `partner-activation-brief.md` - proof-to-partner pitch for processors, wallets, and creator platforms.

## Operating Standard

Only publish proof when it passes these checks:

1. The merchant really accepted DOGE or a DOGE-backed processor route.
2. The proof artifact does not expose private customer information.
3. The post describes the product, merchant, and purchase behavior without implying guaranteed price upside.
4. Any gift-card or processor route is labeled accurately.
5. Campaign copy focuses on adoption, checkout experience, merchant retention, and repeat use.

## Six-Month Metric Target

A credible campaign needs monthly compounding in real usage. Track:

- active accepting merchants,
- DOGE orders,
- gross USD value,
- DOGE settlement volume,
- public proof artifacts,
- repeat buyers,
- retained merchants,
- new merchants recruited from proof posts.

Price is deliberately excluded from the operating scorecard. If DOGE appreciates, that should be a market response to credible adoption, not the campaign message.
