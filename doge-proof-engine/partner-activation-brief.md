# DOGE Proof-To-Partner Activation Brief

## Objective

Use verified DOGE purchase proof to recruit partners that can multiply legal adoption: payment processors, wallet teams, creator platforms, merchant directories, and storefront providers.

This brief should be sent only after the campaign has at least ten verified proof records or three strong category-specific examples.

## Partner Thesis

DOGE does not need a price campaign. It needs a commerce campaign that partners can support without compliance risk:

- buyers spending DOGE on real products,
- merchants seeing orders quickly,
- processors showing invoice volume,
- wallets giving users something useful to do,
- creators converting audience attention into purchases.

## Best Initial Partner Targets

### Payment processors

Target: BitPay, NOWPayments, and DOGE-native builders.

Ask:

- co-host a 30-minute merchant setup sprint,
- provide the shortest DOGE checkout path by platform,
- amplify verified DOGE Spend Week proof,
- help create a DOGE merchant collection or directory tag.

Proof needed:

- at least five verified purchases through that processor,
- screenshots redacted for privacy,
- merchant quotes on setup friction and settlement preferences.

### Wallet teams

Ask:

- feature the DOGE Spend Week directory,
- add an in-app "spend DOGE" prompt during the campaign window,
- amplify proof posts from buyers using their wallet.

Proof needed:

- buyer stories showing wallet-to-checkout completion,
- category examples under $50,
- no investment or price framing.

### Creator platforms and storefronts

Ask:

- pilot one DOGE-ready creator offer,
- feature participating creators,
- test a one-week DOGE checkout or invoice route.

Proof needed:

- creator merch or digital download purchases,
- repeatable offer templates,
- audience comments showing demand to spend DOGE.

### Merchant directories

Ask:

- add a DOGE Spend Week collection,
- mark verified direct DOGE, processor DOGE, and gift-card-route listings clearly,
- link weekly proof reports.

Proof needed:

- clean merchant metadata,
- route labels,
- public proof posts.

## Outreach Email

Subject: Verified DOGE commerce proof for a merchant activation sprint

We are running DOGE Spend Week as a legal adoption campaign focused on real checkout usage, not price promotion.

We now have verified examples of buyers using DOGE for real purchases in [category/route]. The next step is a partner-supported merchant setup sprint: one short onboarding session, one DOGE-ready offer per merchant, and a public proof report at the end of the week.

Would you be open to co-promoting a small pilot with [target number] merchants? We can provide redacted proof artifacts, merchant metadata, compliant social copy, and the weekly adoption report format.

The campaign avoids investment claims, price predictions, coordinated buying, and misleading partnership language. The message is simple: DOGE can be used for real commerce today.

## Proof Packet Checklist

Send partners a compact packet:

- one-page campaign summary,
- three to ten proof records,
- redacted screenshots or receipt artifacts,
- weekly scorecard totals,
- merchant setup path,
- sample social copy,
- compliance guardrails,
- specific partner ask.

## Pilot Standard

Do not ask for broad ecosystem amplification first. Ask for one measurable pilot:

- 10 merchants,
- one DOGE-ready offer each,
- 7 days,
- 50 completed DOGE orders,
- 25 publishable proof artifacts,
- one public results report.

If a partner cannot support that pilot, ask for the next smaller action: a setup guide, directory tag, wallet placement, or one featured merchant.
