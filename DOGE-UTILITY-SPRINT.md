# Dogecoin Utility Sprint

## Objective

Increase the probability of DOGE reaching $1 by driving real payment utility and merchant adoption over the next 6 months. Price cannot be guaranteed, so the only defensible strategy is to increase demand through actual usage, lower integration friction, and a visible merchant pipeline.

## Current facts to anchor on

- The Dogecoin Foundation currently maintains utility-focused building blocks including Libdogecoin, GigaWallet, and Dogebox.
- GigaWallet is positioned as a backend service for shops, exchanges, and platforms that want to accept and transact DOGE.
- Libdogecoin exists specifically to simplify compliant Dogecoin product development.
- Dogebox is explicitly aimed at self-hosted Dogecoin ecosystem software that drives adoption.

## Strategic conclusion

Do not spend effort on generic promotion. Build and distribute a merchant adoption package that turns DOGE acceptance from a custom engineering project into a same-day setup.

The package should have three parts:

1. Merchant acquisition page with a concrete value proposition: accept DOGE in one day, low fees, no bank dependency, global customer reach.
2. Integration kit that compares three implementation tracks:
   - Fastest: third-party gateway
   - Open stack: GigaWallet
   - Deep integration: Libdogecoin
3. Public case-study pipeline showing live merchants, launch dates, and transaction milestones.

## 6-month path

### Month 1

- Launch a single landing page focused only on merchant adoption.
- Publish a "choose your setup path" guide for Shopify, WooCommerce, custom storefronts, and digital creators.
- Create a merchant outreach list segmented into online services, gaming communities, meme-native brands, and international sellers.

### Months 2-3

- Close 5-10 pilot merchants that can publicly announce DOGE acceptance.
- Produce one setup walkthrough per merchant type.
- Publish a simple KPI board: merchants onboarded, checkouts enabled, repeat purchases, total DOGE sales volume.

### Months 4-6

- Expand from pilots to a repeatable playbook.
- Recruit ecosystem partners to co-market the kit.
- Push for integrations where DOGE is culturally native: tipping, gaming, creator memberships, and online merchandise.

## Immediate build target

Create a merchant-facing website and repo assets centered on:

- "Accept Dogecoin in a day"
- clear implementation choices
- proof-of-use instead of price claims
- onboarding forms for interested merchants

## Success metrics

- Number of merchants live with DOGE checkout
- Time from interest to launch
- Number of public case studies
- Repeat customer transactions in DOGE
- Share of integrations using open Dogecoin-native tooling

## What to avoid

- No price promises
- No "get rich" framing
- No market manipulation tactics
- No spend on broad awareness before checkout utility exists
