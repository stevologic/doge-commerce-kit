Why humans choose DOGE

Concrete benefits, not crypto jargon

Fast settlement

Buyers scan, send, and staff can verify in minutes — great for counters, events, and tips.

Low fees

Small sales keep more value than card networks, which matters for coffee, merch, and micro-payments.

No chargebacks

Once your confirmation rule is met, the payment is final — write that policy before the first sale.

Global reach

Anyone with a wallet can pay the same QR — useful for online snippets and traveling customers.
