# Weekly Operations

## Weekly cadence

Every week should produce one of four outcomes:

- new merchants onboarded
- more DOGE transactions
- more public proof of usage
- better retention from prior merchants

If a task does not support one of those, cut it.

## Monday

- review merchant pipeline
- confirm this week's merchant launches
- assign creator visits
- schedule all campaign posts

## Tuesday

- onboard merchants
- verify offer copy and wallet/payment flow
- collect merchant media assets

## Wednesday

- push creator content live
- publish updated merchant list
- post countdown to spend window

## Thursday

- run `Spend DOGE Week` spotlight content
- amplify proof-of-purchase posts
- recruit last-minute customers and partners

## Friday

- feature leaderboard movement
- push strongest offer again
- collect merchant and buyer testimonials

## Weekend

- recap transaction wins
- publish top merchants and creators
- book onboarding calls for next wave

## Weekly targets

- 5 new merchant conversations
- 2 merchant activations minimum
- 10 pieces of user-generated proof
- 1 creator feature
- 1 measurable case study snippet
