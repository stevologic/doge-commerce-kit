# Merchant Playbook

## Positioning

Do not lead with crypto ideology. Lead with business upside:

- attract a new customer segment
- earn free local/social promotion
- run time-limited offers without marketplace fees
- create a memorable brand angle

## Merchant pitch

"We are organizing a DOGE spending campaign that brings paying customers to participating businesses. We provide promotional assets, a community push, and a simple launch flow. Your part is to accept DOGE and offer one small incentive for DOGE payments."

## Best merchant profiles

- owner-operated businesses
- active on Instagram, X, TikTok, or Discord
- already comfortable with digital payments
- willing to test short promotions

## Friction reduction checklist

1. Provide one-page setup instructions.
2. Offer a 15-minute onboarding call.
3. Give them ready-made graphics and captions.
4. Keep the first offer simple.
5. Ask for one staff champion.

## Launch checklist

1. Merchant selects an offer.
2. Merchant confirms payment method/wallet flow.
3. Merchant receives launch kit.
4. Merchant posts launch announcement.
5. Community account amplifies it.
6. Creator visits or features merchant.

## Suggested offers

- "Pay with DOGE, get 10% off this week"
- "DOGE Friday special"
- "DOGE combo bundle"
- "First 25 DOGE customers get a sticker or free add-on"

## Retention tactics

- feature returning merchants in every campaign
- give them leaderboard visibility
- publish short merchant wins
- encourage a standing weekly DOGE offer even between campaign waves
