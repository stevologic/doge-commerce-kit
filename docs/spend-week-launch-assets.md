# DOGE Spend Week Launch Assets

## Landing Page Hero Copy

### Headline

memberships, downloads, tips, and services.

### Primary CTA

### Secondary CTA

Shop with DOGE

## Merchant Pitch

Accept DOGE for one week, get featured in the campaign directory, and turn a
meme audience into actual customers. Keep prices in USD, settle in DOGE at
checkout, and use the campaign to generate visibility plus sales.

## Creator Pitch

Give your audience a reason to spend DOGE now. Offer a limited drop, tip goal,
discount, commission slot, or member perk during DOGE Spend Week and get listed
in the campaign rollout.

## Outreach Templates

### Cold DM to Creator

We're organizing a DOGE Spend Week focused on real purchases, not price talk.
If you can offer one product, tip goal, or member perk payable in DOGE, we'll
feature you in the launch directory and promo threads. Interested?

### Cold DM to Merchant

We're recruiting a small launch group for DOGE Spend Week: stores and digital
sellers willing to accept DOGE for a limited campaign window. We'll drive
attention to participating merchants, provide setup copy, and showcase real
purchase screenshots. Want the starter pack?

### Follow-Up

If setup friction is the blocker, the easiest model is to keep pricing in USD
and let customers pay the DOGE equivalent at checkout. We can position you as a
launch merchant with a single DOGE-ready product first.

## Offer Ideas That Convert

- Limited-run merch colorway only available via DOGE.
- 10% discount for DOGE checkout during campaign week.
- Digital download bundles priced for impulse purchase.
- Commission slots opened only during campaign week.
- Livestream milestone unlocked by DOGE tips.

## Merchant FAQ Angles

- Volatility: quote in USD, convert at payment time.
- Accounting: track sale in USD value at time received.
- Refunds: refund USD-equivalent or DOGE by store policy, disclosed up front.
- Risk: start with one product or one campaign week, not full catalog rollout.

## Launch Standard

Do not launch publicly until there are at least:

- 10 participating sellers,
- 3 creators with active audiences,
- 20 ready-to-use proof graphics or screenshots,
- 1 simple results dashboard for end-of-week reporting.
