# DOGE Spend Week Strategy

## Constraint

No legal strategy can guarantee a market price target. The practical objective
is to create real DOGE demand, visible spend velocity, and repeatable
merchant-creator adoption that can plausibly contribute to a path toward
`$1.00`.

## Core Thesis

DOGE does not need another abstract awareness campaign. It needs a simple,
public loop:

1. Creators ask audiences to pay in DOGE.
2. Merchants and service providers accept DOGE with low friction.
3. Buyers get a visible reason to spend DOGE now instead of only holding it.
4. Every transaction becomes public proof that DOGE is used, not just
   discussed.
5. The proof creates more merchant confidence and more creator participation.

This repo's strongest near-term operating concept is `DOGE Spend Week`.

## Operating Concept

Launch a recurring, creator-led DOGE Spend Week with a merchant starter pack.

### What gets promoted

- "Pay in DOGE this week" offers from small online merchants.
- Creator memberships, digital downloads, commissions, tips, and merch priced
  in DOGE.
- A public proof-of-spend feed highlighting real purchases, not speculation.

### Why this is the best near-term lever

- It is legal and adoption-focused.
- It converts passive holders into active spenders.
- It gives merchants a reason to support DOGE beyond ideology.
- It creates screenshots, testimonials, and transaction stories that spread
  better than technical messaging.
- It can start with a small coalition and still look active.

## Required Assets

1. Merchant acceptance kit
   - One-page setup guide for Coinbase Commerce, BitPay, NOWPayments, or
     self-custody-compatible options.
   - FAQ: volatility, taxes, refunds, pricing updates.
   - Copy-paste website badge: "DOGE accepted here".

2. Creator launch kit
   - Post templates for X, TikTok, YouTube community posts, Discord, Telegram.
   - Offer ideas: paywalled files, shoutouts, custom art, discount codes,
     livestream goals.
   - Rule of thumb: price in USD, settle in DOGE at checkout time.

3. Proof-of-spend page
   - Landing page that aggregates participating merchants, current offers, and
     submitted purchase screenshots.
   - Submission form for merchants and customers.

4. Merchant targeting list
   - Digital-first sellers: gaming communities, meme merch, indie SaaS,
     streamers, artists, AI prompt shops, VPN and privacy niches.
   - Avoid enterprise first. DOGE needs culturally aligned fast adopters.

## Sequenced Plan

### Days 1-7

- Build the landing page and starter kits.
- Recruit 10-20 initial merchants and creators.
- Secure at least 3 visible launch anchors with existing DOGE affinity.

### Days 8-21

- Run the first DOGE Spend Week.
- Post daily proof-of-spend highlights.
- Gather conversion data: orders, gross merchandise value, average order size,
  and repeat buyers.

### Days 22-45

- Publish results publicly.
- Turn the best merchants into case studies.
- Use the case studies to recruit 50 more merchants.

### Days 46-90

- Repeat monthly.
- Add referral incentives for merchants who bring other merchants.
- Add a simple DOGE directory filtered by category and geography.

### Days 91-180

- Position the campaign as a recurring commerce event, not a one-off meme push.
- Pursue wallet, payment processor, and exchange amplification once evidence
  exists.
- Package data into a sponsor deck for ecosystem partners willing to co-market
  adoption.

## Metrics That Matter

- Number of active DOGE-accepting merchants.
- Number of active DOGE-selling creators.
- Monthly DOGE-denominated orders.
- Total DOGE settlement volume.
- Percentage of repeat customers.
- Number of public proof-of-spend posts per campaign.

Follower counts are secondary. Real transaction count is the lead metric.

## Strategic Rules

- Do not market DOGE as guaranteed upside.
- Do not center charts or price predictions in campaign materials.
- Price goods in USD and convert at checkout to reduce merchant fear.
- Focus on niches where buyers already behave online and publicly.
- Treat every merchant success story as sales collateral for the next merchant.

## Highest-Leverage Next Build

If only one thing gets built next, it should be a public landing page that:

- explains DOGE Spend Week in one screen,
- lists participating merchants and creators,
- captures merchant signups and proof-of-spend.

That page is the coordination layer. Without it, promotion is fragmented and
forgettable.
