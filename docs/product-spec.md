# DOGE Commerce Kit Product Spec

## Positioning

`DOGE Commerce Kit` is a merchant onboarding layer for Dogecoin acceptance. It
is not an exchange, wallet custodian, or trading app. The product exists to
make real-world DOGE acceptance easier for small operators and easier to prove
publicly.

## Primary User

The primary operator is a launch team, local organizer, merchant success lead,
or ecosystem contributor trying to onboard real merchants into a DOGE pilot.

The merchant user is usually one of these:

- café or quick-service operator
- creator selling digital goods or tips
- event organizer
- gaming community operator
- local service business

## Problem

Most DOGE adoption discussions are too abstract. A merchant needs a narrow,
practical answer:

- how do I accept DOGE today
- how much training is involved
- how do I reconcile payment
- what should staff do at checkout
- how do I talk about this without sounding speculative

## Product Goals

1. Reduce merchant setup friction to under one hour for simple pilots.
2. Keep merchants in control of funds.
3. Standardize the onboarding package enough that a small team can support many pilots.
4. Generate proof artifacts that can feed public reporting.

## MVP Scope

- landing page and operator console
- market and scenario math
- merchant profile and settlement recommendations
- payment URI and QR generation
- integration manifest generation
- local merchant tracker
- reusable launch templates

## Out Of Scope

- custody of customer or merchant funds
- fiat on-ramp or exchange services
- guaranteed settlement rails
- tax or legal advice
- price or return forecasting

## Future Product Path

After the MVP, the project could expand into:

- Shopify and WooCommerce plugins
- merchant analytics dashboards
- webhook integrations for payment confirmations
- optional fiat settlement partnerships
- city-cluster discovery pages
- recurring proof report publication tooling

## Success Metrics

- number of live merchants
- time to first successful DOGE checkout
- weekly DOGE transaction count
- repeat merchant usage after 30 and 90 days
- number of public proof reports published
