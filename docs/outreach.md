# Outreach Kit

## Merchant Pitch

Subject: Add Dogecoin payments with zero custody changes

Hi `{{merchant}}`,

We are building a public, merchant-first Dogecoin payment push in `{{city}}`.
The ask is simple: add a Dogecoin payment option for one month, report
anonymized transaction counts, and use a public pledge badge so customers know
where DOGE is accepted.

What you get:

- A payment URI and QR invoice generator.
- A public merchant pledge listing.
- Weekly social posts that disclose what is sponsored and what is organic.
- A monthly proof report with transaction counts, not price promises.

Goal: make Dogecoin useful enough that demand is earned in public.

Can we list `{{merchant}}` as an early pledge?

## Creator Brief

Post format:

- Show an actual Dogecoin payment or tip flow.
- Link to a merchant, nonprofit, or creator page that accepts DOGE.
- Disclose sponsorships and holdings.
- Do not promise returns, price targets, or coordinated buys.

CTA:

Use DOGE somewhere real this week, then post the receipt count. Usage is the
message.

## Payment Processor Pitch

We have early merchant interest in Dogecoin payments and are collecting public
proof across transaction count, merchant retention, and repeat usage. We are not
asking for speculative promotion. We want a payment flow that lets merchants
accept DOGE safely, disclose fees clearly, and optionally settle in fiat.

Pilot ask:

- Dogecoin payment link support.
- Merchant reporting dashboard.
- Public case study after 30 days.
- No custody surprises.

## Public Rule

This campaign does not coordinate buys, promise returns, fake volume, hide paid
promotion, or ask anyone to trade. It exists to make Dogecoin easier to use.
